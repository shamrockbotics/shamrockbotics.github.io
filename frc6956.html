Welcome!

Our team was established in August 2017 at Westfield High School by eight FIRST alumni members with the assistance of our school faculty.

Currently, our team is made of eleven students. We also have twelve mentors. Our small numbers made it hard to work on culture projects, programming, and design/hardware/electrical projects all at the same time. We also had a limited budget this year to the point that we had to very diligently watch how much money we were spending in order to not run out. However, we have worked around these challenges and are able to participate in FIRST events.

As an 11 person team, we each had integral roles in both the robot and culture sides of the team. We chose a culture project that we worked on with a mentor each week. We split up the work of designing, programming, building, and wiring the robot. Even though we were all ultimately responsible for our culture project, we each helped others during the busy time of their project. We were fluid in our structure to allow all of us to learn as much as possible.
